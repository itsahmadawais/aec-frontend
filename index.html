Why choose AEC?

Trusted exam centre

We are an Approved National Examination Centre, working closely with all leading awarding bodies, including OCR, AQA, Pearson Edexcel, Cambridge International Examinations, and WJEC. Established nearly a decade ago, we have gained a reputation for quality tuition, great facilities, flexible payments, and a friendly and dedicated team. We are proud of being one of the best-rated exam and tuition centres in the Midlands.
